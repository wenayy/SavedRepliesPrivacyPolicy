
  Privacy Policy — Saved Replies for AI Chats 
                                          
  Last updated: May 11, 2026
                                                                                                                                
  Overview
                                                                                                                                
  Saved Replies for AI Chats ("the extension") is designed with privacy as a core
  principle. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not
  collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal data.
                                          
  Data Collection
                                                                                                                                
  The extension collects no data whatsoever. No personal information, no usage
  statistics, no analytics, no crash reports, no identifiers.                                                                   
                                                            
  Data Storage

  When you save an AI response, the text of that response and the URL of the page
  are stored exclusively in your browser's local IndexedDB database on your own
  device. This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any cloud
  service, not accessible to the developer, and not shared with any third party.                                                
                                              
  Permissions                                                                                                                   
                                                            
  The extension requests the following browser permissions:                                                                     
  
  activeTab — used only to check whether the current tab is a supported AI                                                      
  platform so the popup can display the correct status.     

  tabs — used only to send messages between the extension popup and the content
  script running in the active tab.
                                              
  Host permissions — the extension injects a content script into supported AI
  platforms solely to add bookmark buttons to AI responses. The script reads
  response text only to save it locally to your device when you explicitly click                                                
  the bookmark button.
                                                                                                                                
  Third Parties                                             

  The extension does not integrate with any third-party service, analytics
  platform, advertising network, or API. No data is ever sent outside your
  browser.
                                                                                                                                
  Children's Privacy
                                                                                                                                
  The extension does not collect any data from anyone, including children under
  the age of 13.
  Changes to This Policy
                        
  If the extension is ever updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this
  privacy policy will be updated before that version is published.
